Collaboration

Building relationships based on trust and equitable outcomes


At Outbak Ventures, we believe in building relationships and not merely expanding our network. What that has done is left us with a growing list of associates from whom we can seek guidance and exchange resources. This allows us to collaborate with partners across multiple disciplines and industries around the world on projects that span over a wide variety of domains. This mutual exchange of skills and services helps us grow over time as trusted partners for future projects and also to extend this vast pool of resources to our clients.

We understand that a real association can only happen if it is based on mutual respect and equitable outcomes for all of those involved, and this is the basis we build all our interactions around. From entrepreneurs to investors, our clients at Outbak Ventures can choose to collaborate with any of our associates through us to deal with a particular issue and often in the process of doing so, eventually choose to become one of our associates themselves.

We believe that Outbak Ventures will genuinely become one of the most trusted arenas for cross-dimensional collaboration globally and provide a platform for budding entrepreneurs to kick start their projects.
