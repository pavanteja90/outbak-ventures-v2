Investment Management

Capital optimisation and management to maximise your returns.


At Outbak Ventures, we assume both advisory and executive roles in the management of the investments and capital optimisation. We work with large and small investors and offer a range of services which include valuations, negotiations, mergers and acquisitions, asset management and diversification.

Outbak ventures assist investors to identify new opportunities, markets and products with future potential, allowing them to acquire, invest in or start up their venture here in Australia. We also help startups and budding entrepreneurs to raise capital for their ventures and connect with potential investors or invest in it ourselves on their behalf.

Outbak Ventures also undertake investment management on behalf of investors seeking to become eligible for any of the Business innovation or Investor stream (Permanent) Visa (subclass 888)
